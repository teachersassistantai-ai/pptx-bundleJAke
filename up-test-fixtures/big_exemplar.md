Students MUST include a clearly labelled particle diagram for each state of matter (solid, liquid and gas). A labelled particle diagram for each state is mandatory to achieve a C grade or above. Reports without particle diagrams cannot exceed a D grade.

## Closing note
Teachers should remind students to attach their planning sheet and raw results table to the final submission.

# Supporting Context - Year 7 Science Class

**Prior learning:** Students completed a Year 6 unit on observable physical changes. Most can classify solids, liquids and gases by observable properties but have not yet been introduced to the particle model formally.

**Class profile:** 26 students, mixed ability. Three EAL/D students who benefit from visual supports and sentence starters. Two students with reading support needs require scaffolded text and chunked instructions.

**Available resources:** Class set of thermometers, beakers, ice, hot plates (teacher-operated), data-logging tablets. The school has a particle-model simulation subscription.

**Scheduling note:** The unit runs for 5 weeks, 3 lessons per week. Each lesson is 60 minutes.
